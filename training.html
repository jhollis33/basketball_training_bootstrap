GET TRAINED BY FORMER PLAYERS AND
COACHES ON THE COLLEGIATE LEVEL

Our organization consists of skilled basketball trainers who have played collegiately and/or
professionally. KB focuses on the fundamental development of each player.

For players just beginning to play, we keep things very basic. Our goal with beginners is to ensure they are working on the proper fundamentals. We focus on the correct shooting form and basic ball-handling skills. These skills learned here are a foundation for all of the other skills that they will continue to improve upon. For beginners, we aim to build their confidence and provide a low-pressure environment for them to advance at their own pace.

Fundamentals are still a big focus for players at this level. At the intermediate level, we being to teach more advanced skills and train with game-like situations. Most players at this level are on club or school teams.

Players at this level typically have a full range of skills and we focus more on intense game-like situations in our advanced training program. Players at this level are definitely pushed harder and are trained with the skills needed at the next level.
